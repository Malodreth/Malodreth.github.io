#3  Thinking Again

UPDATE: I wrote another composition called Black Gulch and added it here, as well.


After my last entry, I decided to revisit the old MIDI stomping grounds and give it a new turn — this time with 10 years' more life experience behind me.

I found a new music notation programme called MuseScore 2.0. It's free and open source. 10 minutes in I realised that this software is a million times better than MusicMasterworks (which was a licenced product I was using the trial version for) and supports tablature for stringed instruments!

I dicked around with MuseScore for a couple hours and pieced together a short instrumental. I based main chord progression off of my 2007 MIDI song An Draíocht na Marbh, but ultimately this piece ended up with a slight Aboriginal-Australian/Bluegrass sound to it.

10 years' more experience... decide for yourself whether there's been an improvement. lol
